Team #2: Mavuika Melt

This is is the team I used for when I want to just brute force something. It's a really strong team in general. Mavuika's damage output is still insane even after one year and a half! The team consists of Mavuika herself, Xilonen, Bennett, and Citlali. Mavuika will be doing all the attacking, while Xilonen shreds Pyro resistence and buff Pyro DMG Bonus, Bennett buffs Mavuika's ATK, and Citlali shreds more Pyro resistence and apply Cryo periodically for Mavuika to trigger Melt. It's a pretty cool team too because all of these characters originate from the same region, Natlan! Although, you can technically replace Bennett with Nicole but Nicole is better only when she has her Signature Weapon and I think Bennett belongs here more lol.

Party Members Description

Mavuika 		The God of Pyro herself! As a god of her region, Mavuika have many roles and can fit into several kinds of teams. This time, I'll only cover this Melt team where she's the main DPS. With her Elemental Skill, Mavuika will enter a Nightsoul Blessing state and get a fixed amount of Nightsoul points. Her Nightsoul points will be slowly consumed to maintain this state. While in this state, she has two modes; one is a mode where she summons a ring that deals Pyro damage periodically to nearby enemies, while the other is a mode where literally she summons and ride a motorcycle you can control to traverse and deal damage with. To get the most out of her, she'll need her Elemental Burst. Her Elemental Burst is different than the rest because instead of costing Energy, it costs Fighting Spirit instead. To get Fighting Spirit, you either need to consume Nightsoul Points from characters from Natlan or dealing damage with Normal Attack. The former is the most effective at getting this job done. Luckily, Mavuika and two other units in this team have Nightsoul points that can be consumed to generate Fighting Spirit. Once enough Fighting Spirit has been accumulated, Mavuika can consume them all to unleash her Elemental Burst, entering a special Nightsoul Blessing state and summons her motorcycle to deal a ginarmous damage. While in this special state, Mavuika's Nightsoul points will not be consumed for a period of time, and her motorcycle's damage will be increased based on the amount of Fighting Spirit consumed. It's very important that you trigger Melt when doing damage with her motorcycle because by doing so, the damage dealt will be increased by literally double the amount. Basically, she's a nuke! Her one and only artifact set is Obsidian Codex. Other damage increasing set is fine but nothing beats a set that buffs DMG Bonus AND Crit Rate.

Bennett 		A Pyro Support unit that still work great for carries ever since the game's release! They didn't call him "Broken da Broken" for nothing! Not to mention he's F2P friendly and very easy to use. Just simply unleash his Elemental Burst to create a field that increases the on-field unit's ATK by a huge amount and heals when they have low enough HP. His best artifact set is Noblesse Oblige. A simple character, yet very strong!

Xilonen 		A Geo Support unit that debuffs enemies with a side of sustain. With her Elemental Skill, Xilonen will enter a Nightsoul Blessing state and gains a fixed amount of Nightsoul Points. Her Nightsoul points will be slowly consumed to maintain this state. To activate her debuffs, she needs to reach the maximum amount of Nightsoul points she can have. To do that, simply deal damage with her Normal Attack while in this state to gain a big amount of Nightsoul points. Once she has enough, All of her Nightsoul points will be consumed, and her debuffs will be activated, shredding the enemies' Geo, Pyro, Hydro, Electro, and or Cryo resistence depending on the element of the units in the party. Her best artifact set varies. In this case, she'll use Archaic Petra to buff Pyro DMG Bonus by picking up a Pyro Crystallized shield. Her Elemental Skill is basically all you need but if you're running low on HP, her Elemental Burst can be used as a panic button to heal your on-field units.

Citlali		 A Cryo Support unit that grants sustainability, debuffs, and periodic Cryo application. With her Elemental Skill, she'll summon Itzpapa, grants a shield, and enter a Nightsoul Blessing state and gains a fixed amount of Nightsoul points. If she has enough Nightsoul points, Itzpapa will be angy and slowly consume the points to periodically apply Cryo on to the nearby enemies. To gain more Nightsoul points, Citlali can use her Elemental Burst. Additionally, when Itzpapa is active, any party members that trigger Melt or Frozen will shred nearby enemies' Pyro and Hydro resistence. With Scroll of the Hero of Cinder City artifact set, Citlali'll buff everyone with Pyro DMG Bonus by triggering Melt as well as Cryo DMG Bonus. Citlali buffs, debuffs, and applies periodic Cryo, then Mavuika will eat those buffs and debuffs and apply Pyro to trigger Melt and increases her damage. Simple, right?
