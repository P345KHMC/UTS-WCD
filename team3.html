Team #3: Pyro Traveler Lunar Overvape

This is a team I completely made up in my head but it gives me a clear on Fearless Stygian so it should be good right? Anyway, this is a Pyro Traveler DPS team with Columbina, Ineffa, and Xilonen. The initial idea was to give my Traveler a regional team for Nod-Krai and this team fits the most while also being good. To be fair, every DPS can be good with Columbina and Ineffa on the team. As everyone calls it, the "Bineffa wheelchair" team. These two, especially Ineffa, deals so much damage even though they're off-field characters. They can catch up, even surpass the on-field DPS, which is why they're called wheelchairs lol. Xilonen is the cherry on top, buffing everyone's damage in the team. Unfortunately, I don't have any artifact set that buffs Lunar Reaction damage so the team's full potential has not been reached yet.

Party Members Description

Columbina		 A Hydro unit and a goddess that holds authority over the three moons' power. With her power, she'll convert some Elemental Reactions into their Lunar variant, including Electro-Charged, which will summon a thundercloud that deals periodic Electro damage to enemies when the reaction is triggered. With her Elemental Skill, she'll summon a Hydro ring that deals damage and apply Hydro periodically to enemies that come into contact. Also, when her Elemental Skill is active, if you trigger a certain amount of Lunar Reactions, she'll deal a direct Lunar Reaction damage depending on the Lunar reaction that's been triggered the most. In this team, Lunar Electro-Charged (Or Lunar-Charged) will be triggered the most. Therefore, Columbina will deal a direct Lunar-Charged damage. With her Elemental Burst, Lunar Reactions will be buffed to deal more damage. For her artifact set, I think maximizing her personal damage is important, so Aubade of Morningstar and Moon is the way to go. But I could only give her 2Pc HP and 2Pc Elemental Mastery set sadly.

Ineffa 		An Electro unit that may or may not carry the team the most lmfao. Her Elemental Skill summons Birgitta that deals periodic Electro damage and provide a shield. While Birgitta is active, if a thundercloud created by the Lunar-Charged reaction is nearby, Birgitta will deal additional damage that's considered a Lunar-Charged damage. THIS is what makes Ineffa deals so much damage because the scaling is actually crazy lol. With her Elemental Burst, she'll deal an instance of Electro damage and buffs herself and the on-field unit with Elemental Mastery. Same as Columbina, maximizing her damage output is a must so give her the Aubade of Morningstar and Moon artifact set as well. Since I don't have it, I give her the Gilded Dream set, which is a usable set for her.

Xilonen 		A Geo Support unit that debuffs enemies with a side of sustain. With her Elemental Skill, Xilonen will enter a Nightsoul Blessing state and gains a fixed amount of Nightsoul Points. Her Nightsoul points will be slowly consumed to maintain this state. To activate her debuffs, she needs to reach the maximum amount of Nightsoul points she can have. To do that, simply deal damage with her Normal Attack while in this state to gain a big amount of Nightsoul point. Once she has enough, All of her Nightsoul points will be consumed, and her debuffs will be activated, shredding the enemies' Geo, Pyro, Hydro, Electro, and or Cryo resistence depending on the element of the units in the party. Because she's in a reaction-focused team, It's recommended to give her the Silken Moon's Serenade artifact set to increase everyone's Elemental Mastery, increasing reaction damage such as Lunar-Charged, Overload, and damage dealt by Vaporize. The Instructor set can be used if you don't have it. Her Elemental Skill is basically all you need but if you're running low on HP, her Elemental Burst can be used as a panic button to heal your on-field units.

Pyro Traveler		 If you've played Genshin for a while, you would expect that all of the Traveler's elemental kit to be uhh... not so good. Thankfully, Their Pyro kit is surprisingly decent! They can either be used as an off-field Pyro applicator or an on-field DPS with C6. I'm gonna cover the latter. With his Elemental Skill, Pyro Traveler will enter a Nightsoul Blessing state and gain a fixed amount of Nightsoul points. Their Nightsoul points will be slowly consumed to maintain this state. To make this state last longer, Pyro Traveler can gain Nightsoul points by triggering any Pyro-related reaction while in this state or by unleashing Elemental Burst. However, they'll exit this state after a certain duration. After entering this state, Pyro Traveler will either summon a Pyro ring that will deal a small damage to enemies that come into contact or a drone-like thing that'll target a specific enemy that has been hit by the on-field unit once every a certain amount of time depending on if the Elemental Skill was tapped or hold. Either one is fine so just pick your favorite. The real fun part is when Pyro Traveler is C6 because with it, while in a Nightsoul Blessing state, Pyro Traveler's sword will be infused with Pyro and they'll get DMG Bonus along with it, allowing them to be an on-field DPS! As the cherry on top we have their Elemental Burst, which is basically a nuke. Try unleashing it while the enemies are affected by Hydro to trigger Vaporize, increasing this nuke damage even more! Because Pyro Traveler consumes Nightsoul Points, Obsidian Codex is their best artifact set as an on-field DPS.
