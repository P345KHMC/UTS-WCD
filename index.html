Team #1: Varka Heat Wave

This is my top favorite team in Genshin. Mostly because the party members resembles the role in DnD (Dungeons and Dragons). We have Durin the Dragon, Venti the Bard, Nicole the Storyteller, and Varka the Knight. Not only that, but the characters in this team know each other and serves important roles in the game's story! It's just so cool. Aside from the aesthetics, this team is a pretty strong and comfortable one. We have a Pyro Sub-DPS that deals DMG off-field and shred Pyro and Anemo resistance, a really strong crowd controller that provides DMG Bonus for the main DPS, a shielder that can buff ATK for everyone, and the main DPS himself. It's a complete package, except for the fact that it doesn't have a healer but the shield from the shielder is strong enough to avoid the issue of taking damage.

Party Members Description

Durin 		A Pyro Sub-DPS unit that deals damage off-field using his Elemental Burst. His Burst has two modes; Light mode and Dark mode. In this team, he'll use Light mode to shred Pyro and Anemo resistance by triggering Swirl with any unit in the team. His best artifact set is A Day Carved From Rising Winds. This set will maximize his damage output. However, I prefer using the Celestial Gifts set on him to buff both himself and Varka with Pyro and Anemo DMG Bonus.

Venti 		An Anemo unit that can either be a Support or a Main DPS. For this team, he'll be a Support. By using the Viridescent Venerer artifact set on him, he'll shred a significant amount of the enemies' Pyro resistance by triggering Pyro Swirl to increase Varka's damage output as well as Durin's. With his Elemental Burst, he'll create a Stormeye that pulls nearby enemies in for a certain duration. Also, when a unit on the field triggers Swirl while Stormeye is active, it'll grant them DMG Bonus, which is very good for Varka.

Nicole 		A Pyro Support unit that grants sustainability and ATK buffs. By using her Elemental Skill, Nicole will summon a shield, which absorbs damage taken by the on-field unit and let them be resisted from interruption. Her Elemental Skill also buffs everyone in the party with ATK buff, increasing the damage dealt by them, and even more for the on-field unit. By using the Thrilling Tales of Dragon Slayers weapon on her, she can buffs Varka with even more ATK. Her Elemental Skill is the most important part of her kit, but there's also her Elemental Burst. It'll give her a stack of certain buff that can be consumed by dealing damage with the on-field unit. By consuming the buff, it'll summon a projection attack, dealing damage based on the element and the stat of the on-field unit who unleash it. Her preferred artifact set is Celestial Gifts but if Durin is already equipped with that set, then she uses the Noblesse Oblige set to increase more ATK for everyone in the party.

Varka 		Our main DPS! Varka is an Anemo DPS unit that wields two claymores to deal Anemo damage with one claymore and either Pyro, Hydro, Electro, or Cryo damage with the other depending on the element of the units in the party. Because there are Pyro units in this team, he will deal Pyro damage. By using his Elemental Skill, Varka will enter a state that infuse both of his claymores with Anemo on one and Pyro on the other. His Elemental Skill will also change during this state. By dealing damage with his Normal Attack during this state, he will decrease his Elemental Skill cooldown. When his Elemental Skill is ready, Varka can unleash two different attacks depending on if you press the Elemental Skill button or use his Charged Attack. Pressing the Elemental Skill button will let him deal two instances of huge damage, one of which is Pyro damage and the other Anemo damage. Meanwhile, using his Charged Attack instead will let him do two cross slashes, dealing two instances of Pyro damage and two instances of Anemo damage. It's recommended to press the Elemental Skill button because the execution is faster than using his Charged Attack. As for his artifact set options, he has plenty of decent ones. A Day Carved From Rising Winds is his best in slot, but any set that buffs his ATK or DMG Bonus is fine too!
